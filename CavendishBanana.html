Cavendish Banana

  • Cavendish bananas are the fruits of one of a number of banana cultivars belonging to the Cavendish subgroup of the AAA banana cultivar group.
  • These yellow bananas are the most widely used and classic banana variety that you've grown up with
  • Cavendish have been the staple lunchbox variety since the late 1950s, and for good reason!
  • This variety has year-round availability, is disease resistant, and features a classic banana flavor.
